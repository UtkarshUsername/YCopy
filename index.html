    Save, then copy in one tap

    How it works:

    1. Save a clip: tap + or share to YCopy.
    2. Copy it back: tap a saved clip to copy to your clipboard.
    3. Organize: long-press to select multiple, then pin/share/delete.

    Tip: use Search to filter fast.

    Settings

    Pinned clips are never auto-cleared by time and are excluded from max count. When max is reached, oldest unpinned clips are removed first.
